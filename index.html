Hi.

I'm Anthony, a senior studying Informatics at the University of Washington.
If you are interested in knowing more about me, read on!

I'm a Taiwanese who grew up in the Bay Area, and now based in Seattle.
Some hobbies - I read a lot of Chinese light novels to keep my Mandarin up to date.
I've been playing Go for nearly 2 decades, and Badminton from the beginning of high school to the end of college.
And I'm somewhat close to turning professional in PUBG Mobile.

I spend plenty of my time doing non-coding stuff, though when I do code I enjoy it just as much as my other hobbies.
When I code, I have a perculiar passion in architecting and designing solutions from the ground up.
I figured I enjoy taking ownership, and the process of placing the puzzle pieces on the right track - slowly but surely.
Recent experiences also gave me a lot of opportunities to make design-based decisions - my way of thinking is slowly gearing to be more design-oriented, rather than implementation focused.
As for specific field I'm interested in, I'm still exploring.
On the other hand, though, I also get very satisfied from solving Leetcode brainteasers.
